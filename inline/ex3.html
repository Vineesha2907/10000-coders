SAMANTHA

Samantha was born on 28 April 1987, to Joseph Prabhu and Ninette Prabhu. She was born in a Christian family.[4] She was born and brought up in Chennai, Tamil Nadu,[5] as the youngest child of the family, with two elder brothers Jonath and David.[6][7] Samantha did her schooling at Holy Angels Anglo Indian Higher Secondary School Chennai and then completed a degree in commerce at Stella Maris College, Chennai.[6][8] Towards the end of her undergraduate degree she became involved in modelling, notably working with Naidu Hall, through which she was first spotted by filmmaker Ravi Varman


RAKUL PREETH SINGH


Rakul Preet Singh (born 10 October 1990) is an Indian actress who predominantly works in Telugu, Hindi and Tamil films. Singh has won several awards including a South Indian International Movie Award, in addition to four nominations at the Filmfare Awards South.[2][3] Singh made her acting debut with the Kannada film Gilli (2009). She forayed to Telugu and Tamil cinema with the films Keratam (2011), Thadaiyara Thaakka (2012) and Venkatadri Express (20113). She went on to appear in several films in Tamil and Telugu including Loukyam (2014), Pandaga Chesko (2015), Sarrainodu (2016), Dhruva (2016), Nannaku Prematho (2016), Rarandoi Veduka Chudham (2017), Spyder (2017) and Theeran Adhigaaram Ondru (2017).
